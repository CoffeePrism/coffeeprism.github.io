Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a contributor?

We welcome writers who are passionate about coffee to join our team. If you're interested in contributing content to Coffee Prism, please contact us using the methods above and include your writing samples or relevant experience.

Can I recommend products?

Absolutely! We greatly appreciate product recommendations from our readers. If you have a favorite coffee or coffee equipment you'd like to share, please let us know through the contact form, and we'll consider adding it to our recommendation list.

Can I repost your content?

Our content is licensed under the MIT License, which means you can repost it as long as you credit the source. For commercial use, please contact us first to obtain permission.

How do I report website issues?

If you find any problems or errors on the website, please let us know through the contact form. We'll fix these issues as soon as possible.
