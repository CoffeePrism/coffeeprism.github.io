Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly.

Can I contribute content to Coffee Prism?

Yes! We welcome contributions from coffee enthusiasts. Please use the contact form and select "Partnership Opportunity" as the subject. Include details about your expertise and content ideas.

Do you offer coffee consultations?

We offer limited coffee consultations for businesses and serious enthusiasts. Please contact us with details about your needs, and we'll let you know if we can help.

How can I report an issue with the website?

If you encounter any issues with our website, please use the contact form and select "Website Feedback" as the subject. Include details about the problem, such as the page URL, your device, and browser.
