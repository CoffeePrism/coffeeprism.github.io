# About Coffee Prism

Coffee Prism is a site dedicated to coffee culture, knowledge, and gear. We help coffee lovers explore the world of coffee through in-depth, practical guides — every recipe comes with concrete parameters you can actually reproduce at home.

This English edition is a pilot: a hand-curated selection of our most useful evergreen guides, editorially adapted (not machine-translated) for international readers.

## Our Mission

Just as a prism splits light into its full spectrum, our mission is to break coffee down into its many facets — origin, processing, brewing, and science — so you can understand and appreciate every layer of what's in your cup.

## What We Cover

Coffee Prism focuses on four core topics:

1. **[Coffee beans](/en/coffee-beans/)** — how origin and processing shape what different beans taste like
2. **[Brewing methods](/en/brewing-methods/)** — step-by-step technique guides with exact ratios, temperatures, and timings
3. **[Equipment reviews](/en/coffee-equipment/)** — hands-on evaluations of grinders, brewers, and other coffee gear
4. **[Coffee and health](/en/health/)** — what the research actually says about coffee and your body

## Our Team

We're a small team of coffee-obsessed professionals — baristas, roasters, and coffee researchers. We keep learning and testing so that everything we publish is accurate, current, and genuinely useful.
