Coffee Processing Methods

How Processing Affects Flavor

The way coffee cherries are processed after harvesting significantly impacts the final flavor profile of the beans. Different methods highlight different characteristics of the coffee.

Washed Process

Removes all fruit before drying, resulting in clean, bright flavors with higher acidity.

Natural Process

Dries the whole cherry around the bean, creating fruity, sweet, and complex flavors.

Honey Process

Removes the skin but leaves some fruit mucilage, balancing acidity and sweetness.
