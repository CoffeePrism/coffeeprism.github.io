Equipment Care & Maintenance

Extending the Life of Your Coffee Equipment

Proper maintenance not only extends the lifespan of your coffee equipment but also ensures consistent brewing quality over time. Regular cleaning and care prevent the buildup of coffee oils and residue that can affect flavor.

Grinder Maintenance

Clean burrs regularly, use grinder cleaning tablets monthly, and consider annual burr replacement for heavy use.

Espresso Machine Care

Backflush regularly, descale according to water hardness, and replace gaskets and shower screens as needed.

Brewer Cleaning

Rinse thoroughly after each use and deep clean with appropriate cleaning solutions periodically.
